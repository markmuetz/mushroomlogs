About The Mushrooms

We supply the finest "ready to fruit" mushroom logs from Oak Farm in the Heart of the Waveney Valley on the Norfolk / Suffolk Border. All our Mushroom Logs are English Grown Sustainable Oak thinnings; inoculated with carefully selected Shiitake mushroom strains; then matured for up to 24 months so they are ready to produce multiple annual crops of delicious mushrooms.

Harvest

Our amazing logs will start producing shiitake mushroom crops in the next few months and will continue to produce 1-4 crops a year for up to 5 more years! They make the most fantastic gifts for the urban and country gardener alike and provide years of bountiful mushroom crops. We also offer an array of other fun and fascinating Christmas Gifts and Stocking Fillers including: Truffle Trees, Mushroom Book Recycler kits, DIY mushroom log kits, and Pocket Gardens.
